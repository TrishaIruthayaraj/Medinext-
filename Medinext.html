                Ophthalmic Equipment Manufacturing Units
            

            

                Our various manufacturing facilities in Chennai (Tamil Nadu), Pondicherry, Kolkata (West Bengal) and Baddi (Himachal Pradesh) are EN ISO 13485 certified and CE approved by notified bodies of European Union.
